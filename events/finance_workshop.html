Bridging the Gap

The ACM Student Chapter of VIT Chennai, in collaboration with VITFAM (Finance and Management Club), successfully conducted a Personal Finance Workshop. The session focused on equipping participants with the essential knowledge and practical tools required to make informed financial decisions in their personal and professional lives.

The workshop covered a range of important topics including budgeting, saving, managing expenses, understanding credit and loans, and the fundamentals of investments. It emphasized the importance of early financial planning.

Foundations of Finance

Introduction to financial literacy essentials: Budgeting, Saving, and Managing Expenses effectively as a student.

Credit & Liabilities

Understanding credit scores, how loans work, and the importance of avoiding bad debt early in your career.

Investment Fundamentals

An overview of investment vehicles, highlighting the power of compounding and long-term stability.

Psychology of Spending

Interactive discussions on disciplined financial behavior and how to connect concepts to everyday scenarios.
