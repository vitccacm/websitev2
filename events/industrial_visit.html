Executive Report

The event was an offline-online hybrid networking session featuring representatives from ACM India. As part of their nationwide tour visiting ACM Student Chapters, the delegation conducted an interactive session focused on research landscape and professional growth.

The primary agenda was to discuss research opportunities within ACM, as well as broader ACM India and international operations. Students gained direct insight into global team interactions and publication avenues.

Key Outcomes

  • Direct interaction with the Global ACM Team.
  • Guidance on high-impact publication opportunities.
  • Networking for Research Scholars (Offline) and Students (Online).
