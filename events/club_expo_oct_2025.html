Event Overview

The ACM Student Chapter at VIT Chennai actively participated in the Club Expo, presenting the chapter's objectives, initiatives, and technical culture to incoming students. We engaged visitors through interactive coding games, live demonstrations, and instances developed by club members, offering a clear insight into ACM's emphasis on practical learning and innovation.

Engagement & Activities

Informative discussions highlighted opportunities for skill development, research exposure, and collaborative projects within the chapter. The event successfully generated strong interest, encouraged new registrations and recruitments. It positioned ACM as a professional and impactful community for aspiring computing enthusiasts.

Impact

Through interactive sessions and hands-on demonstrations, we successfully conveyed ACM's mission of advancing computing as a science and profession. The event attracted significant attention from freshers looking to develop their technical skills and contribute to meaningful projects.
